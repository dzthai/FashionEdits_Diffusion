Summary

FashionDiffusion is a generative modeling project that explores the use of diffusion models to create novel fashion designs conditioned on visual and stylistic attributes. We curated and preprocessed a dataset of apparel images, then trained a denoising diffusion model to learn the underlying distribution of garment structures, colors, and textures. The system enables controlled generation of new clothing concepts by guiding the model with specific prompts or attribute embeddings, demonstrating how modern generative techniques can support creative workflows and rapid design prototyping. This project highlights the application of deep learning, computer vision, and large-scale data preprocessing in a real-world, creative domain.
